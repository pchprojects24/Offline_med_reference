Breathing Procedure

Chest tube insertion (tube thoracostomy)

Indications

  • [Placeholder: Pneumothorax (traumatic, spontaneous, iatrogenic)]
  • [Placeholder: Hemothorax]
  • [Placeholder: After needle decompression for definitive management]
  • [Placeholder: Empyema, large pleural effusion]

Contraindications / cautions

  • [Placeholder: Coagulopathy - correct if possible, but do not delay in emergency]
  • [Placeholder: Diaphragmatic injury suspected - consider imaging first if stable]
  • [Placeholder: Adhesions from prior surgery - increased complication risk]

Equipment

  • [Placeholder: Chest tube (appropriate size)]
  • [Placeholder: Scalpel, forceps (Kelly clamp)]
  • [Placeholder: Suture material]
  • [Placeholder: Sterile drape, gloves, antiseptic]
  • [Placeholder: Local anesthetic]
  • [Placeholder: Chest drainage system]

Steps (checklist)

  1. [Placeholder: Position patient - arm above head, lateral decubitus or supine]
  2. [Placeholder: Identify safe triangle (4th-5th ICS, anterior axillary line)]
  3. [Placeholder: Prep and drape]
  4. [Placeholder: Anesthetize - skin to pleura]
  5. [Placeholder: Make skin incision (~3cm) at ICS below insertion site]
  6. [Placeholder: Blunt dissect through chest wall over rib]
  7. [Placeholder: Finger sweep to confirm pleural entry and clear adhesions]
  8. [Placeholder: Insert tube, direct posteriorly and apically]
  9. [Placeholder: Connect to drainage system]
  10. [Placeholder: Secure tube with suture, apply dressing]

Confirmation / success criteria

  • [Placeholder: Misting/bubbling in drainage system with respiration]
  • [Placeholder: Drainage of air/blood/fluid]
  • [Placeholder: Improved breath sounds]
  • [Placeholder: Improved respiratory status]
  • [Placeholder: CXR confirmation when feasible]

Complications + immediate management

  • [Placeholder: Malposition - reposition or replace]
  • [Placeholder: Lung injury - usually self-limited, monitor]
  • [Placeholder: Intercostal vessel injury - may require surgical intervention]
  • [Placeholder: Infection/empyema - antibiotics, may require surgical drainage]
  • [Placeholder: Subcutaneous emphysema - ensure tube is functioning]

Aftercare / monitoring

  • [Placeholder: Continuous drainage monitoring]
  • [Placeholder: Serial CXR]
  • [Placeholder: Monitor for re-expansion pulmonary edema]
  • [Placeholder: Pain management]
